About

Hi I'm Andy — The App Dad

Yorkshire dad. Hobbyist iOS builder. Problem solver by necessity.

Chosen was born out of a very real standoff in our house — choosing our baby's name. Every idea was either "absolutely not" or "I'll think about it." It started to feel less magical and more like a negotiation. So instead of arguing about it… I built something.

I create apps the way I live life — practical, honest, and designed to make things simpler. Most of my coding happens between naps, after bedtime, or whenever I can steal an hour.

No big team. No big funding. Just building things that fix problems I've personally felt. If it helped me, there's a good chance it'll help you too.
