Common Questions

What happens when I delete my account?

It removes your data from your devices and deletes your account from our servers.

How do I delete my account?

Open the menu (top left) and choose Reset app → Delete Account.

How do I reset my swipes and scores?

Open the menu (top left) and choose Reset app. This clears data on this device.

How do I connect with a partner?

Tap Share Invite and send the code to your partner. They should enter the code on their device.
