Include your iOS version, app version (Settings → App details), a short description of the issue, and screenshots if helpful. We aim to respond within a few business days.

Frequently asked questions

Where is my data stored?
All claims and receipt images are stored locally on your device. There is no account or cloud sync in the current version.

How do I restore MyExpenseHub Pro?
Open Settings → MyExpenseHub Pro → Restore purchases. Use the same Apple ID as the original purchase.

OCR picked the wrong amount or currency
OCR is a best-effort helper. Edit the amount, date, or currency before saving. Ensure the total line is clearly visible in the photo.

How do I export a claim?
Open a claim → tap the share/export icon → choose PDF or CSV.

How do I delete a claim or receipt?
Swipe left on a receipt or claim, tap Delete, and confirm for claims.

The free version shows ads
Upgrade to MyExpenseHub Pro in Settings to remove ads and unlock multi-currency features.

Purchases and refunds

MyExpenseHub Pro is sold through the Apple App Store. Billing, receipts, and refunds are handled by Apple — use Report a Problem or Apple Support for payment disputes.

System requirements

iPhone or iPad running iOS 17.6 or later. Camera recommended for receipt capture; Photo Library import is also supported.
